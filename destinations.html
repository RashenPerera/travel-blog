Top Travel Destinations in Sri Lanka

Sigiriya – The Lion Rock Fortress

Sigiriya is an ancient rock fortress with stunning views and historic frescoes.

  • Climb to the summit
  • Explore Mirror Wall and frescoes
  • Walk through water gardens
  • Visit Sigiriya Museum

Ella – Hill Country Haven

Ella is a peaceful mountain town surrounded by tea plantations and hiking trails.

  • Hike Little Adam’s Peak
  • See Nine Arches Bridge
  • Visit Ravana Falls
  • Take scenic train ride

Galle – Colonial Charm by the Sea

Galle is a coastal city with colonial architecture and vibrant street life.

  • Walk Galle Fort ramparts
  • Visit lighthouse and museum
  • Explore shops and galleries
  • Relax at Unawatuna Beach
